Evapotranspiration

Definition of ET

Evapotranspiration is the flux of water from vegetative surfaces and water bodies into the atmosphere. It is an important part of water balance.

Fig: The amount of water per year that precipitates, evaporates, runs off into streams and rivers, or soaks into groundwater storage for each of seven main land masses.
